Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to set up my AI assistant?

Most assistants are live within 5 to 7 business days. This includes analyzing your business, training the AI, testing responses, and deploying to your chosen channels.

Will the AI give incorrect answers to my customers?

Your AI is trained exclusively on your business data -- your products, services, pricing, and FAQs. It only answers what it knows. If a query is outside its knowledge, it politely escalates to a human team member.

Can I use the AI assistant on both WhatsApp and my website?

Yes. The Starter plan includes one channel of your choice. The Professional plan includes WhatsApp, website chatbot, and social media -- all for one price.

What happens if I want to cancel?

You can cancel anytime with no penalties. There are no long-term contracts. Your data and conversation history remain available for export for 30 days after cancellation.
